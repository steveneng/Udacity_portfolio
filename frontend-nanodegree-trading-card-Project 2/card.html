Red Panda

Red Pandas have a surprising taste for fake surgar. An recent experiment showed that Red Pandas prefer water sweetened with artificial sweeteners.

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Average Length: 64 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Temperate Forests

Red Pandas have a reddish brown fur with a long tail. They are slightly larger than a domestic cat. Red Pandas are endangered from habitat destruction and poaching.
